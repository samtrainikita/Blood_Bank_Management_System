Frequently Asked Questions

Who is eligible for Blood Donation?

Anyone can donate blood who are in good health.Donors must weigh at least 110 pounds (about 50 kilograms).Be at least 17 years old (16 with written consent from a parent or guardian).Previous donation must be done 3-4 months before.

What are the benefits of blood donation?

It helps in the generation of new blood cells which in turn refreshes the body.Donating 450 ml of blood burns 650 calories. However, this does not mean one can donate every other week. Men can donate once in three months, while women can do so once in four months. Regular blood donation, especially for males is said to reduce the risk of heart disease. It helps to keep a check on iron levels in the body. An increase in the level of iron in your blood could lead to heart disease or increase the chances of heart attack.

Is it mandatory for all to donate blood?

It is purely on voluntary basis. Anybody can donate their blood by registering themselves through ICSI e-Blood Portal.

Is it mandatory to Register first for donating blood?

Yes it is mandatory to register first for donating blood

Is Email and Mobile number both are mandatory while registration?

Yes as it is being used by the user to contact donors.
